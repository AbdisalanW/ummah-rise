Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

No accounts, no names. Ummah Rise does not ask for your real name, phone number, or an account login. Every post is shown under a randomly generated anonymous name that is not linked to your identity.

What we store. The content of posts, replies, and Taasiya shares you submit, plus counts like likes and duas. This is stored in our database to display the community feed.

Donations. Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not see or store your card details. Stripe's own privacy policy governs that payment step.

Claiming funds. If you attach a fundraising goal to a post, you receive a one-time claim code. Claim codes are never shown back to you or anyone else after creation, look-ups happen through a secure server-side check, not a public database read.

Claiming your funds. If your fundraiser reaches its goal and you want to withdraw the money, you'll complete a short signup directly with Stripe, our payment processor, to verify your identity and bank account. That information goes to Stripe, not to us. We never see or store your bank details, government ID, or legal name.

Push notifications. If you allow notifications, your browser creates a subscription tied only to the specific post you made, not to a personal identity, so we can notify you of likes, replies, duas, or donations on that post.

On your device. Your anonymous name, your own post history, and which posts you've reported are stored only in your browser's local storage, not on our servers.

Data requests. Because posts are anonymous, we cannot look up "your" data by identity. If you want a specific post removed, contact us with the post content or claim code and we will remove it.
